### Run Instructions

#### Controls

- W A S D to move
- Space to Jump

#### Mechanics

- Defeat enemies by jumping on them
- Defeat all plant enemies to clean off the fountain
- Pick up Orb rewarded for cleaning the fountain to win
- Lose by running out of health from enemy attacks
